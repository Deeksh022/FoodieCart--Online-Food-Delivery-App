How do I track my order? +
You can track your order in real-time through the 'My Orders' section in the Swiggy app or website. We'll also send you SMS updates.
What is Swiggy's cancellation policy? +
You can cancel your order for free before the restaurant starts preparing your food. After preparation starts, a cancellation fee may apply.
How do I apply a coupon code? +
Go to your cart and click on 'Apply Coupon'. Enter your coupon code or select from available offers. The discount will be applied automatically.
